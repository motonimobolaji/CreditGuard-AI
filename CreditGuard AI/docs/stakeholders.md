Stakeholder Analysis
All potential stakeholders in my project.

Fraud Analyst
Fraud analysts are the primary users of the CreditGuard AI platform. Their responsibility is to investigate suspicious credit card transactions and determine whether they are legitimate or fraudulent. Because financial institutions process thousands of transactions every day, manually reviewing each one is impossible. CreditGuard AI supports fraud analysts by assigning a fraud risk score to every transaction and prioritizing the highest-risk cases for investigation. This allows analysts to spend their time on the transactions that are most likely to be fraudulent, improving efficiency while reducing financial losses. Their feedback can also help improve future versions of the fraud detection model.


Customer (The cardholder)
Customers are directly affected by the decisions made by the fraud detection system. Their primary need is for legitimate purchases to be approved quickly while fraudulent transactions are blocked before financial damage occurs. An overly aggressive fraud detection system may incorrectly decline legitimate purchases, causing frustration and reducing trust in the financial institution. Conversely, a weak fraud detection system may allow fraudulent transactions to go undetected. CreditGuard AI aims to balance fraud prevention with a smooth customer experience by improving detection accuracy and minimizing false positives.


Risk Management Team
The Risk Management Team oversees the financial institution's exposure to fraud and operational risk. They use fraud analytics to identify emerging fraud trends, evaluate the effectiveness of fraud prevention strategies, and make recommendations to reduce financial losses. CreditGuard AI provides valuable insights through fraud risk scores, trend analysis, and performance metrics that help the team make informed decisions about risk policies and fraud mitigation strategies. Their objective is to reduce losses while maintaining efficient business operations.


Banking Executives
Bank executives are responsible for making strategic decisions that improve profitability, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency. Rather than reviewing individual transactions, executives rely on dashboards and summary reports to understand overall fraud trends and the effectiveness of fraud prevention efforts. CreditGuard AI provides executive-level dashboards that summarize key performance indicators such as fraud rates, estimated losses prevented, model performance, and transaction trends. These insights support data-driven decisions regarding investments in fraud prevention technologies and operational improvements.


Data Scientist / Machine Learning Engineer
The data scientist or machine learning engineer is responsible for designing, developing, evaluating, and maintaining the fraud detection model. Their goal is to ensure that the model accurately identifies fraudulent transactions while minimizing false alarms. They analyze data quality, engineer predictive features, compare machine learning algorithms, monitor model performance, and retrain models as fraud patterns evolve. CreditGuard AI serves as both a predictive system and a platform for continuous model improvement through explainable AI and performance monitoring.


Compliance and Regulatory Team
Financial institutions operate under strict regulations designed to protect customers and ensure fair decision-making. The Compliance and Regulatory Team ensures that fraud detection systems follow applicable financial regulations and internal governance policies. They require transparency in how fraud decisions are made, documentation of model development, and evidence that customer data is handled securely and ethically. CreditGuard AI supports these requirements by incorporating explainable AI techniques, maintaining documentation throughout the project, and emphasizing responsible use of predictive analytics.


Information Technology (IT) Operations Team
The IT Operations Team is responsible for deploying, maintaining, and supporting the technical infrastructure behind the fraud detection platform. Their responsibilities include ensuring system reliability, database performance, application availability, and data security. CreditGuard AI must integrate smoothly with existing systems while maintaining high availability and secure handling of transaction data. A reliable platform allows fraud analysts and business stakeholders to access accurate information whenever it is needed.


Merchants
Merchants are businesses that accept credit card payments from customers. They are indirectly impacted by fraud because fraudulent transactions often result in chargebacks, financial losses, and damaged customer relationships. Although merchants do not directly interact with CreditGuard AI, they benefit from improved fraud detection because fewer fraudulent purchases result in fewer disputes and lower operational costs. Merchant transaction patterns also provide valuable information that helps the model identify potentially fraudulent behavior across different industries.
